What is Bananagrams?

Imagine scrabble but freeform and more chaotic.

The objective of the game is to be the first person to use all the
tiles you're given to create one big contiguous scrabble board.

There are a couple different mechanics in the game play:

  1. Peel

    2. Peel is what you call when you use all the letter tiles in your hand.

    It will give all players in the game another tile.

    You can only peel when all of your tiles are connected to one gameboard

    and all words are valid in the dictionary.

  2. Dump

    3. Dumping a tile is a choice players have to swap out one tiles in their

    hand for 3 more random ones from the remaining tiles.

    This does not affect the other players directly; only that it takes

    tiles from the shared source.

  3. Bananas

    4. Bananas is called when you think you've won!

    Your board will then be checked to see if all words are valid and connected.
